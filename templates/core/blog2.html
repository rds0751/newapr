7 benefits of Digital Signatures you didn’t knew!

DIGITAL Signatures are Good

We have now seen how Digital signatures works and but let us explore the benefits it brings to us:

Digital certificates are small files on devices with file extension .cer mostly. It establishes the relationship between user and public. It is standardized by PKI. It is issued by some trusted party always.

The Certificate Authority (CA) are trusted bodies that can issue digital certificates.

A digital certificate contains information such as:

  • 1. Enhanced security: One of the most promising feature of digital signatures is that it enhances security. It uses identifying fingerprints which is unique to every document, which helps to detect whether the given document has been tampered or not. It also uses encryption verification technology which is called PKI which is very helpful in verifying the user by the signature.
  • 2. The second benefit can be that the digital signatures are being accepted and used worldwide now. Many companies and are now exchanging documents through digital signatures. “The Electronic Signatures in Global and National Commerce Act of 2000” has been newest addition to the Digital Signatures.
  • 3. The digital signatures cannot be touched or tampered by any third party and they are verified independently. Moreover, the process of getting a digital signature is not very complex and can be easily done.
  • 4. The manually signed documents can be easily misplaced, damaged and stolen.
    • Digital signatures are the new technology that would revolutionize the distribution of documents, there is no doubt why people think digital signatures is a good idea. The manually signing of documents was also wasting a lot of papers, on the other hand, the digital signatures are ecofriendly and also it is more secure than the manually signed documents. The manually signed documents pose a threat to theft and also can be very easily tampered.
  • 5. They are easy to use ad require no rocket science to use them.
  • 6. Even if the person is not present there, and there is an emergency for signing the document, Digital signatures provide a solution to it. The person can sign the document anywhere even with a mobile phone.
  • 7. Finding documents between so many files are a very time-consuming task, but using digital documents, we can find it with just one click.

The Certificate Authority (CA) are trusted bodies that can issue digital certificates. Users send request to CA for digital certificate. After the CA verifies the information of the user ,it send certificate to the user keeping a copy for itself.
